Counselling on ANC

Before each ANC Visit

Remember to:

  • Wash your hands before and after examination

  • Ensure a well lit room

  • Talk to woman/partner with respect

  • Ensure privacy and confidentiality

Prepare Inventory/Check list:

  • Examination gloves

  • Vaginal examination tray (examination gloves, antiseptic povidone iodine or hibitane, ring forceps or sponge holding forceps, speculum, cotton swabs or gauze, lubricant)

  • Clean sheet

  • Alcohol rub

  • Functional BP apparatus, weighing scale, foetal stethoscope, tape measure and test kit (hemoglobin, urine, malaria)

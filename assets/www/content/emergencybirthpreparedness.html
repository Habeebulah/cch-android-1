Emergency Birth Preparedness

There may be complications that cannot be handled at the nearest facility.
  1. Make arrangements for transport to higher level facility.

  2. Set aside money and/or make payment arrangements with vehicle owner.

  3. Identify a compatible blood donor

  4. Prepare money for referral in case you are referred to another facility

  5. Identify someone who would accompany you if you are to be moved from one facility to another.
